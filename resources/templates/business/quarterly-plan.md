## Progress Review Cadence

### Weekly Check-in

**Day**: Monday 10:00 AM
**Duration**: 30 minutes
**Attendees**: Initiative owners + leadership
**Focus**:
- Initiative status (on track / at risk / blocked)
- Blockers and resolution
- Resource needs

### Monthly Review

**Day**: Last Friday of month
**Duration**: 1 hour
**Attendees**: Full team + stakeholders
**Focus**:
- OKR progress (current vs target)
- Budget burn rate
- Risk assessment
- Adjust priorities if needed

### End of Quarter Review

**Day**: [Final week]
**Duration**: 2 hours
**Attendees**: Full organization
**Focus**:
- Final OKR assessment
- Lessons learned
- Next quarter planning kickoff
